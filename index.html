Data Deletion Instructions

If you would like to request the deletion of your data associated with our app, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to privacy@yourapp.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".
  2. Include your Facebook User ID in the email. You can find your User ID by visiting this link.

Once we receive your request, we will process it within 30 days and notify you once your data has been deleted.

If you have any questions, please contact us at privacy@yourapp.com.

For more information, please review our Privacy Policy.
